How the AI coach works

3 simple steps from chaotic workouts to a system that adapts to you.

1

Connect your devices

Connect your smartwatch, sleep tracker, or just your phone. We privately collect key signals like heart rate, daily activity, sleep, and stress levels.

2

AI analyzes biometrics & mood

Every day, our algorithm quietly evaluates your energy level, mood, and recovery to understand what you can realistically handle today—no pressure.

Instead of a rigid program, you get a living plan: short sessions, micro‑workouts, recovery days, and gentle nudges when it's a good time to move.
