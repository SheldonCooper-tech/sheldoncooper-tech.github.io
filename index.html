How the AI coach works

3 simple steps from chaotic workouts to a system that adapts to you.

Step 1. Connect your devices

Connect your smartwatch, sleep tracker or just your phone. We collect key signals like heart rate, activity, sleep and stress.

Step 2. AI analyzes your biometrics and mood

Every day, the algorithm evaluates your energy level, mood and recovery to understand what you can realistically handle today.

Step 3. You get an adaptive workout plan

Instead of a rigid program, you get a living plan: short sessions, micro‑workouts, recovery days and gentle nudges when it’s a good time to move.
